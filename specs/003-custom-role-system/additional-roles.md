## Grey Team Characters

### Grey Team
**Base Role**

**Description:**
You must exchange your card for a Red Team card or a Blue Team card at some point during the game. When you switch, you assume the allegiance and win condition of your chosen team. If you fail to exchange by game end, you lose.

**Pros:**
- Can choose which team to join based on game state
- Strategic flexibility

**Cons:**
- Must remember to exchange before game ends
- No natural allies until you choose

---

### Changer (Grey Team)
**Pair:** Custom "Fun Deck"

**Win Condition:**
You must exchange your card for a random card from a custom "Fun Deck" at some point before game end. This can only happen once unless you draw another Changer card.

**Lose Condition:**
If you forget to exchange, you lose.

**Pros:**
- Adds randomness and excitement
- Can potentially get powerful roles

**Cons:**
- Requires custom deck setup
- High risk of forgetting to change

**Recommended With:** Custom variant decks

---

### Illuminati (Grey Team)

**Description:**
Your card has both red and blue sides. You can display either side when revealing.

**Win Condition:**
If any player card shares with you, you win and all other players lose.

**Pros:**
- Can pretend to be either team
- Powerful win condition

**Cons:**
- Everyone loses if you win
- May make you a target if suspected

**Recommended With:** Characters that encourage card sharing

## Special Power Characters

### Alien
**Type:** Card Share Power
**Team:** Blue Team ‚≠ê

**Power - ALIEN:**
When you card share with a player, you may steal their card and give them yours. Both players assume the powers and allegiance of their newly acquired cards.

**Pros:**
- Can steal powerful roles
- Strategic card manipulation

**Cons:**
- May backfire if you steal a bad role
- Requires careful timing

**Recommended With:** Privacy Promise rule variant

---

### Alchemist
**Type:** Card Share Power
**Team:** Blue Team ‚≠ê

**Special Team Win Condition:**
When playing with the Alchemist, the Blue Team has the following additional win condition: the King must card share with the Alchemist before the end of the game or the Blue Team loses. At the end of the game, the King will be asked if they card shared with the Alchemist.

**Note:** This is similar to Doctor/President relationship but for King variants.

**Pros:**
- Adds strategic depth to King variant games
- Creates important targets for information gathering

**Cons:**
- King must find and share with Alchemist or team loses
- Games with Alchemist and Engineer may lead to all players losing

**Recommended With:** King, Fat Princess, Apprentice

---

### Beholder
**Type:** Information Power
**Team:** Can be any team

**Power - BEHOLDER:**
You can see all card shares and color shares that happen in your room. Players must show you their cards when they share with others in your presence.

**Pros:**
- Massive information advantage
- Natural spy catcher

**Cons:**
- May become a target once discovered
- Requires players to remember to show you

**Recommended With:** Games with 15+ players

---

**Type:** Card Share Power
**Team:** Blue Team ‚≠ê

**Power - CLEANER:**
Any player that card shares with you has specific negative conditions removed (e.g., "cursed", "bitten", etc.).

**Note:** Similar to Medic but may have different condition removal rules.

**Cons:**

### Seer
**Type:** Information Power
**Team:** Can be any team

**Power - SEER:**
You witness all card shares and private reveals that happen in your room. Players must privately show you their cards when they reveal to others.

**Pros:**
- Exceptional information gathering
- Can identify key players

**Cons:**
- May become high-value target
- Difficult to track all reveals

**Recommended With:** Games with 15+ players

### Trader
**Type:** Card Share Power, Card Swap
**Team:** Can be any team

**Power - TRADER:**
When you card share with another player, you may offer to swap cards with them. If they agree, you both trade cards and assume the new allegiances and powers.

**Note:** Unlike Hot Potato, this requires mutual agreement.

**Pros:**
- Strategic card swapping with consent
- Can negotiate favorable trades

**Cons:**
- Requires convincing others to trade
- May reveal your card is undesirable

**Recommended With:** Privacy Promise rule variant

## Backup Characters

### Apprentice
**Type:** Bury, Card Share Power
**Recommended Pair:** Alchemist
**Team:** Blue Team ‚≠ê

**Description:**
You are the backup character for the Alchemist. If the Alchemist card is buried, you must carry out Alchemist responsibilities (i.e., card sharing with the King).

**Pros:**
- Feel the power of being an alchemist if the Alchemist card is not in play

**Cons:**
- Feel like a regular Blue Team member if the Alchemist card is in play

**Recommended With:** King variant games

---

### Fat Princess
**Type:** Bury
**Recommended Pair:** King
**Team:** Blue Team ‚≠ê

**Description:**
You are the backup character for the King. If the King card is buried, you must carry out all King responsibilities.

**Note:** This is the King-variant equivalent of President's Daughter.

**Pros:**
- You are the heir, a huge deal if the King card is buried

**Cons:**
- Just another Blue Team member if King is active

**Recommended With:** King, Red Fist

---

### Blue Firecracker

**Team:** Blue Team ‚≠ê

**Description:**
You are the backup character for the Blue Drone. If the Blue Drone card is buried, you assume their role and responsibilities.

**Pros:**
- Backup for specialized Blue Team role

**Cons:**
- Depends on Blue Drone being buried

**Recommended With:** Blue Drone variant games

**Type:** Color Share Power
**Team:** Can be any team

**Pros:**
- Players will be less willing to color share with you

**Cons:**
- May make you unpopular
- Reveals your card more often
